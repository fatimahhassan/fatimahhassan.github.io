Lakehouse Hotel

.An exclusive retreat according to old English tradition, the Lakehouse Hotel is a colonial bungalow at Ringlet offering 19 luxury rooms and suites. Located along the main road about 2km after Ringlet town or 5km before Tanah Rata, it's one of the most famous boutique hotels of Cameron Highlands that houses an exquisite fine dining continental restaurant. MORE

Strawberry park resort

Another popular family hotel in Cameron Highlands, Strawberry Park Resort lies shut up in the hilly surroundings off Tanah Rata, perched on a hill slope surrounded by tranquil rainforest. Located about 10 to 15 minutes away from either Tanah Rata or Brinchang town, the resort provides luxury accommodation in boutique suite rooms.MORE

Casa Dela Rosa

Casa dela Rosa is a boutique hotel nestled on a hill slope along the main road at Tanah Rata, with a view overlooking the golf course (or garden & rainforest). Catering for family accommodation, the rooms are well appointed and tremendously spacious for many units. The hotel is a convenient five minutes away from either Tanah Rata or Brinchang town.MORE

Nova Highland Resort & Residence

Nova Highlands Resort & Residence is a family hotel providing apartment-based accommodation at Aranda Nova, a commercial and urban development at Kea Farm about 3km north of Brinchang town along the main roadThe hotel also features Cameron Square - the only shopping mall in Cameron Highlands with convenient facilities and eateries.MORE

Heritage hotel

Heritage Hotel is a family resort with a Tudor-inspired facade, nestled along the ridge of a large hill that overlooks Tanah Rata town, accessible from a slip road along the main road after the shophouses. Offering boutique accommodation with a wealth of facilities, the location here is incredibly scenic and peaceful, covered by the occasional blanket of mist.MORE
