Sri Juliana Villa

Sri Juliana Villa is a cluster of modern bungalows with slight mock Tudor architecture, perched on a hill slope overlooking Brinchang town center, just a short distance away from its restaurants and shops. Each villa provides cozy boutique accommodation in a contemporary setting, with spacious living hall, full kitchen facility and attached bathrooms with long baths. With a limited number of rooms, the resort is an ideal private getaway for couples or a small family, with a nice garden space and breezy surrounding greenery. Nearby tourist attractions the guests can walk to include Taman Agro Tourism (a large strawberry farm) and Cactus Valley. MORE

Natasya Resort

Natasya Resort is a budget hotel comprised of low-rise studio apartments at Copthorne Hotel (formely Equatorial) in Kea Farm, a few km away from Brinchang town. Each family apartment comes with a living hall, kitchenette with microwave oven and fridge, spacious private balcony with sun lounger, and bedroom with king or twin beds - along with the sofa bed the room can sleep an extra or even two persons. Also, there are two bathrooms with long baths and shower in each room - one for the bedroom and the other in the living hall. While the hotel lacks facilities, free wifi Internet access is provided for each room. Finally, the famous vegetable market is a short walking distance away.MORE

Hotel Titiwangsa

Hotel Tititwangsa is a large budget inn proving family accommodation, facing the main road just before the steep slope that leads directly into Brinchang town center. While catering mostly for families, the hotel also has rooms for double and triple occupancy, along with complimentary Wifi Internet access. The front terrace along the ground floor is occupied by a small shopping arcade; counting among the shops a convenience store, bakery, cafe, restaurant, fashion boutique and health center (foot reflexology). Facilities at the basement floor of the hotel include three conference halls and a Chinese steamboat buffet restaurant. Finally, a small bar with private karaoke rooms provide some entertainment at night.MORE

Hotel jasmine

Hotel Jasmine is a budget family hotel among the shophouses of Brinchang town center, just five minutes away on foot from the popular weekend night market. Providing accommodation in 72 comfortable rooms equipped with satellite television, hot water showers and complimentary wifi Internet access, the hotel is surrounded by numerous restaurants and convenience shops below for convenience. Rooms come in configurations for twin, triple and quad occupancy, with the largest room type being the family suite (also for quad occupancy but more spacious and luxurious setting). Finally, tour and taxi services are available from the reception counter. MORE
