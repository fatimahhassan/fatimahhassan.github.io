Cameron Valley

The Kuala Terla Cameron Valley Tea House (GPS: 4.54535, 101.42097) is one of three Cameron Valley tea houses operated by the Bharat Group. It is located in Kuala Terla, on the northern part of Cameron Highlands. The tea house is along the main road, with the tea plantation located immediately behind it. Of the three Bharat Group tea houses, this one receives the least visitors, on account of it being far away from the other two, and the scenery here is less panoramic. You can find us on Google Map
