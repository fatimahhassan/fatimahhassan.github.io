Sri Juliana Villa

Sri Juliana Villa is a cluster of modern bungalows with slight mock Tudor architecture, perched on a hill slope overlooking Brinchang town center, just a short distance away from its restaurants and shops. Each villa provides cozy boutique accommodation in a contemporary setting, with spacious living hall, full kitchen facility and attached bathrooms with long baths. With a limited number of rooms, the resort is an ideal private getaway for couples or a small family, with a nice garden space and breezy surrounding greenery. Nearby tourist attractions the guests can walk to include Taman Agro Tourism (a large strawberry farm) and Cactus Valley.
