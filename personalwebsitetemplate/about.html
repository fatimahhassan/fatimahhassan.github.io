Sri Juliana Villa

Explore the Cameron Highlands. Stay in the heart of Brinchang at the foot of Mt Brinchang, only 5 minutes walk to shops, restaurant and the famous weekend night market. The location is superb and very central to all the happening places that one would want to visit.

Our private modern villas overlooking the bustling Cameron Highlands village of Brinchang. This is a fantastic base to explore all the Cameron Highlands has to offer. Sit back, sip a hot drink and watch the village life from your balcony. Cameron Highlands is not just a popular tourist destination, but hoe to a busy plantation and agricultural industry. Soak up the atmosphere behind the scene of the main tourist destination.
