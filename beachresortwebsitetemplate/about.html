about

Rose apartment

Rose Apartment is a new condominium at Kea Farm in Brinchang, providing budget family accommodation through several hotel operators, mostly in three bedroom apartment units with beds for at least eight pax, and a kitchen with stove and fridge. An LCD television set with satellite channels is usually standard, along with hot water in the bathrooms, but Wifi Internet access is limited. A private balcony comes attached with every unit. To reach Rose Apartment from Brinchang town, head towards Kea Farm's vegetable market along the main road (just before Equatorial Resort), then turn in through the stalls and people. The road leads directly to Rose Apartment at left, just before the Rose Center.

Desa Anthurium

Desa Anthurium is a cluster of low-rise family apartment blocks at Tanah Rata, located just before the golf course a short drive away after passing through town. Nestled slightly off the main road, the apartment also provides quick access to Brinchang, with a quiet and peaceful location fringed by hilly jungle and plantation scenery. Decked with a light blue facade and Tudor-inspired architecture, Desa Anthurium provides budget accommodation in three bedroom apartments and penthouse units, with public facilities that include car park lots and a barbeque area at the center courtyard. To reach the apartment, drive along the main road from either towns, and keep an eye out for the buildings, which will be immediately visible.

Equatorial Hill

Equatorial Hill Resort comprises low-rise and tower-type condominium blocks nestled around Copthorne Hotel at at Kea Farm. Just a few km away from Brinchang town, the apartments reside on a incredibly panoramic ridge that overlooks the valley of farms and plantations, providing spacious budget family accommodation with a good range of facilities such as working kitchens, satellite television and complimentary wifi Internet access for some units. Tourist attractions such as the nearby vegetable market, Butterfly Garden and Butterfly Farm are located within quick walking distance from the hotel, while a variety of shops, restaurants and strawberry farms can be found within the vicinity.

Parkland apartment

Parkland Apartment is a budget hotel providing family accommodation in one and three bedroom condo units, nestled on a tranquil plateau that overlooks Brinchang town. Simple and pleasant, each apartment comes with a living hall, kitchenette and balcony, while bedrooms come with attached bathrooms and queen or single beds. The elevated position of the hotel and surrounding rainforest provides a calming getaway away from the hustle and bustle of town. Also situated on the grounds are small gardens with little gazebos and benches for guests to relax. Located off the main road, the hotel can be reached by turning left at the signboard just before Brinchang town.
