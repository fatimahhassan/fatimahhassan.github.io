Tourist Attraction

  • mardi agro park

    Getting there : travel via the road next to Century Pines Hotel/children playground, pass the football field. it is located somewhere on the left side of the road on the the way to Arabella apartment.

    • AdultRM 5.00
    • ChildrenRM 3.00

  • big red strawberry farm

    Getting there: Driving down via the Brinchang Point you will find a little narrow entrance. This strawberry farm shares the same location with another nursery called Cactus Valley.

    • Entrance FREE
    • pluck strawberry (one box)RM 30.00

  • cactus valley

    Getting there: If following the main road, turn left immediately after Star Regency Hotel (the Brinchang night market site will be opposite the hotel). Follow the road until you see the signboard at right to Cactus Valley or Big Red Strawberry Farm; a quick but steep uphill climb later and the entrance pops to view.

    • Adult and ChildrenRM 5.00

  • rose centre

    Getting there: Rose Center is a flower garden at Kea Farm, located off the main road about 3.5km away from Brinchang town. Visitors can reach this tourist attraction by following the road that leads through Kea Farm Market until Rose Center appears at right side of the road

    • AdultRM 3.00
    • ChildrenRM 1.50

  • butterfly garden

    Getting there: nestled along the main road 3km after Brinchang town or 5km from Tanah Rata (just down the road from Copthorne Hotel).

    • AdultRM 3.00
    • ChildreRM 1.50

  • time tunnel

    the museum occupies a basement within Kok Lim Strawberry Farm, perched next to the main road shortly after leaving Brinchang town towards Kea Farm

    • AdultRM 5.00
    • ChildreRM 3.00
